Constants and enums¶

Constants and enums are listed here.

Constants¶

FZ_STORE_DEFAULT¶
 A reasonable upper bound on the context size.
FZ_STORE_UNLIMITED¶
 Let the context size unbounded.
fz_device_bgr¶
 Abstract colorspace representing device specific bgr.
fz_device_cmyk¶
 Abstract colorspace representing device specific CMYK.
fz_device_gray¶
 Abstract colorspace representing device specific gray.
fz_device_rgb¶
 Abstract colorspace representing device specific rgb.
fz_expand_images¶
 Expand images when writing document.
fz_expand_fonts¶
 Expand fonts when writing document.
fz_expand_all¶
 Expand images and fonts when writing document.
fz_empty_bbox¶
 An empty bounding box with an area equal to zero. Both the top left and bottom right corner are at (0, 0).
fz_empty_rect¶
 An empty rectangle with an area equal to zero. Both the top left and bottom right corner are at (0, 0).
fz_identity¶
 Identity transform matrix.
fz_infinite_bbox¶
 An infinite bounding box with negative area. Corner (x0, y0) is at (1, 1) while corner (x1, y1) is at (-1, -1).
fz_infinite_rect¶
 An infinite rectangle with negative area. Corner (x0, y0) is at (1, 1) while corner (x1, y1) is at (-1, -1).
fz_unit_bbox¶
 A bounding box with sides of length one. The top left corner is at (0, 0) and the bottom right corner is at (1, 1).
fz_unit_rect¶
 A rectangle with sides of length one. The top left corner is at (0, 0) and the bottom right corner is at (1, 1).
