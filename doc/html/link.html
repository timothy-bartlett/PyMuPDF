Link¶

Represents a link to somewhere (in the document, other documents or the internet). Links are by document page, and they are chained to each other, starting from an initial link which is accessible by the page.loadLinks() method.

Methods¶

rect() Get the hot zone. The area that can be clicked in untransformed coordinates.
get_kind() Get the kind of link destination.
get_uri() Get a UTF-8 encoded URI to launch.
get_page() Get the target page number of a internal link
get_page_flags() Get the validity and meaning of a internal link, see fz_link_flag
get_page_lt() Get the top left corner of the destination bounding box.
get_page_rb() Get the bottom right corner of the destination bounding box.

get_page_flags(self)¶

Get the validity and meaning of a internal link, see fz_link_flag.

The flags is a bitfield consisting of fz_link_flag describing the validity and meaning of the different parts of lr and rb. Link destinations are constructed (as far as possible) so that lt and rb can be treated as a bounding box, though the validity flags indicate which of the values was actually specified in the file.

See also
